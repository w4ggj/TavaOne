FCC Approves Limited Emergency Use of 70 cm Band by AST SpaceMobile Satellites Outside the US

Source: ARRL News

Big news for the 70 cm crowd — the FCC has given AST SpaceMobile the green light to use the 430–440 MHz band for emergency telemetry and control on their constellation of 248 large satellites, but only when they're not over the US. It's worth keeping an eye on how this develops, as any commercial encroachment near our bands is something hams should stay informed about.
