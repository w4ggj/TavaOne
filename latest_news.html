FCC Approves Limited Emergency Use of 70 cm Band by AST SpaceMobile Satellites Outside the US

Source: ARRL News

This is a big one to keep an eye on, folks — the FCC has given AST SpaceMobile the green light to use the 430–440 MHz band for emergency satellite telemetry and control, but only when the birds are outside the US. It's limited for now, but our 70 cm band is getting some new neighbors and the amateur community will want to stay tuned as this constellation of 248 large satellites gets rolling.
