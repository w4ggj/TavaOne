FCC Approves Limited Emergency Use of 70 cm Band by AST SpaceMobile Satellites Outside the US

Source: ARRL News

This one's worth keeping an eye on, folks — the FCC has given AST SpaceMobile the green light to use the 430–440 MHz segment for emergency satellite telemetry and control, but only when their birds are outside the US. It's limited for now, but any commercial encroachment on our 70 cm band is something we should all be paying attention to.
