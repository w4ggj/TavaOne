The ARRL Solar Update

ARRL News

Solar activity has cooled off this past week, with mostly C-class flares coming out of Region 4465 — including a C2.6 on June 10 as the week's biggest event. If you've been working FT8 DX and the bands feel a little flat lately, that's probably why — fingers crossed things pick back up before Field Day weekend!

ANS-158: HADES-SA Designated Spain-OSCAR 127 (SO-127)

AMSAT News

Fresh from the AMSAT bulletin this week — the HADES-SA satellite, also known as SpinnyOne, has officially been designated Spain-OSCAR 127, adding another bird to the amateur satellite fleet. Also a reminder that the deadline for AMSAT Board of Directors nominations is June 15, so if you're into satellite ops and want to get involved, now's the time.
