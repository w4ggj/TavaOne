The ARRL Solar Update

ARRL News

Solar activity has cooled off this past week with mostly C-class flares coming out of Region 4465 — not ideal if you've been chasing DX or hoping for some long-haul FT8 magic on the upper bands. Keep an eye on the flux numbers heading into Field Day weekend and hope the sun cooperates!

Find the Right Rig: New Comparison Tool for ARRL Members

ARRL News

ARRL just dropped a handy new member benefit — the QST Product Review Comparison Database — that lets you compare transceivers side by side using data pulled straight from QST reviews. If you've been on the fence about your next rig purchase, this tool could save you a lot of spreadsheet headaches.

ANS-158: HADES-SA Designated Spain-OSCAR 127 (SO-127)

AMSAT News

A new OSCAR designation is always exciting news — HADES-SA, also known as SpinnyOne, has officially been designated Spain-OSCAR 127 (SO-127). Also worth noting: the deadline for AMSAT Board of Directors candidate nominations is June 15, so if you're involved with the satellite side of the hobby, now's the time to pay attention.
