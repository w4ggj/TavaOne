What Is WSPR and How Can It Improve Your Station? (Video)

OnAllBands

If you're into digital modes like FT8, WSPR is worth having in your toolkit — it's a weak-signal protocol that lets you check propagation and evaluate your antenna performance without even needing another ham to be listening on the other end.
